Account Deletion & Data Removal

Warning: Deleting your account is a permanent action. All your profile data, recent searches, and personal settings will be permanently erased.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the permanent deletion of your dotko.in account and all associated personal data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to trust@dotko.in from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Set the subject line to: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered GSTIN (if applicable) and your Business Name in the body of the email.

What Happens Next?

Upon receiving your validated request:

  • Your account access will be suspended within 48 hours.
  • All personal identity markers, profile data, and login credentials will be wiped from our primary Firebase Authentication database.
  • Please note: Any aggregated, anonymized trade reports you submitted against third-party business profiles will remain in the system to protect the integrity of the trust scoring engine, but your identity will be permanently unlinked from them.
